EchoSpeech
  • Paired device control
  • Voice command recognition in noisy environments
  • Control of devices where hand-held control is unreasonable
  • Acoustic vibration sensing via sonar
  • Deep learning algorithm to recognize commands
  • Bluetooth connection for external device control
  • Wearable device based off eyeglasses
  • Interfaces with program on smartphone or laptop
  • User-controlled data
  • User information is processed on the connected device, not in the cloud
  • Integrates with smartphone apps, such as calendars and music applications
  • Integrates with drawing and CAD programs
  • Requires only a couple minutes of training before it can be used for the commands in the model
  • Easily connects to any device with Bluetooth
  • User only needs to wear the device, and the onboard sonar and AI algorithm does the rest
  • 95% accuracy, based on echo profiles of lip and mouth movement, making it perfect for somebody with a speech impediment or similar situation
  • No physical interaction is needed, as the passive sonar scanning automatically detects commands in real-time
  • Low-energy technology used, resulting in long battery life
  • Individuals in a high-sound environment who value privacy and convenience for controlling their devices and data
  • Individuals who may have difficult vocalizing sounds or speech impediments

Voiceitt

  • Deep learning for processing commands
  • Statistical modeling and analysis

  • Very limited privacy-policy - large amounts of data are collected and sold

  • Integrates with most common smart-home personal assistants

  • Designed to be used for those whose speech patterns do not fit typical patterns
  • Highly accessible to anybody with a smartphone
  • Limited-time free trial of application functionality
  • Those with speech impediments and/or abnormal speech organs
  • Those with heavy accents that atypical in relation to the language that they are speaking
